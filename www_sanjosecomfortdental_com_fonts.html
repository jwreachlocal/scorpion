San Jose Comfort Dental

Also Serving Residents of Santa Clara, Los Gatos & Campbell, CA

Our team at Comfort Dental strives to provide patients with outstanding dental care, a comfortable atmosphere, and affordable dental services. We have the ability to help with a wide variety of dental needs, and our dentists use state-of-the-art dental technology. We are so confident with their work that we guarantee a majority of our dental services for at least seven years. We are located in Silicon Valley, conveniently situated between Santana Row and Valley Fair and proudly serve patients throughout San Jose, Santa Clara, Los Gatos and Campbell, California. Our fully-equipped staff is compassionate and always makes it their priority to listen to our clients' wishes and concerns, and we aim to meet every client's end goal.

Dentistry Services in San Jose, California

During a client's first visit, we take a full health history and will perform a complete cleaning. We use special instruments that scrape below the gum line to remove plaque and tartar, which may cause gum disease. Our dentists' goals are to help patients maintain exceptional oral health, and to prevent problems by identifying and treating issues as soon as they arise. Depending on age, risk of disease, and symptoms, we may recommend x-rays, which may find problems that go unnoticed, such as impacted teeth or tumors. Our dentists use x-rays that emit virtually no radiation, but as a precaution, we still use lead aprons.

What Comfort Dental Can Offer Patients

We may assist patients with somewhat simple procedures—such as fillings and gum treatments—as well as perform more specialized oral treatments, such as implants, root canals, Invisalign, laser teeth whitening, and wisdom teeth removal. When a person has a cavity or lost tooth structure that cannot be restored by a filling, our San Jose dentists have the ability to provide patients with inlays and onlays. These can be used to replace most back teeth fillings, and are often cosmetically preferred over regular fillings. Most gum diseases may be prevented by scheduling regular checkups with a dentist; however, if a person believes that they have a gum disease, our dentists may perform the necessary treatment to repair their condition.

If a client is missing teeth, and wishes to restore the natural appearance of their smile, Dr. Askari, may recommend dental implants. Most people with adequate bone mass can have implants, although it varies among individuals. Implants do not have the disadvantages of a fixed bridge or removable dentures, and they do not affect adjacent teeth. A root canal may need to be performed on a client if a cavity has gone untreated, and there is an infection in the pulp. Depending, we may be able to treat it in one doctor's visit.

Our office also offers Invisalign to patients who do not wish to have braces. Invisalign are clear, smooth aligners that are virtually invisible and easy to remove for cleaning. Unlike braces, Invisalign products offer individuals a comfortable method to straighten teeth and give them the smile they always wanted. If a patient is suffering from enamel discoloration, we can perform laser teeth whitening, which may reverse the staining. We may also remove a patient's wisdom teeth if they are impacted and causing discomfort.
